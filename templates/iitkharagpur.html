IIT Kharagpur (IIT Kgp) offers degree and dual degree programmes at the undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG) and doctorate (PhD) level. The institute offers programmes majorly in the streams of Engineering and Science. However, IIT Kharagpur also offers Architecture (BArch), Management (MBA, EMBA), Law (LLM) and Medical Science & Technology programmes (MMST). IIT Kharagpur BTech Admission Minimum Eligibility Criteria 2018 Admissions to BTech programmes at IIT Kharagpur is a three step process. Aspirants need to meet the eligibility requirements for JEE Main and JEE Advanced which includes criterions like the candidate’s Nationality, age, performance in JEE Main, number of attempts in the exam, appearance in Class XII etc. For the detailed eligibility of JEE Main and JEE Advanced click the respective links given above.
IIT Kharagpur BTech Admission and Selection Process 2018 IIT Kharagpur BTech admissions process. 
  Step 1: The first step is to qualify JEE Main. Top 2.4 lakh engineering aspirants who clear this exam will be eligible for JEE Advanced.
  Step 2: The second step is to appear for JEE Advanced provided aspirants clear JEE Main. Admissions to BTech programmes at IIT Kharagpur will be based on the rank acquired in JEE Advanced.
  Step 3: After the declaration of JEE Advanced results, eligible candidates are required to appear for JoSAA (Joint Seat Allocation Authority) online counselling. The process typically commences in the month of June every year. IIT Kharagpur BTech Application Process 2018 Eligible students - besides applying for JEE Main and JEE Advanced – will also have to register for JoSAA counselling.
