Admission:

Birla Institute of Technology (BIT), Jharkhand (also known as BIT Mesra) offers degree and integrated programmes to aspirants at the undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG) and doctorate (PhD) level. Programmes offered at BIT are in streams including – Engineering, Architecture & Planning, Medicine & Health Sciences, Hospitality & Travel, Science, Business & Management Studies, IT & Software as well as Animation.
Minimum eligibility criteria for all courses
For admissions to UG programmes at BIT Mesra, applicants must have passed Class 12 / Intermediate or equivalent qualifying examination with a minimum of 45% to 45% (5% relaxation for SC/ST) marks in Class 12 / Intermediate or equivalent qualifying examination, depending on the course. Appearing students can also apply.
Eligibility criteria that aspirants need to fulfill to secure admission in PG programmes offered at BIT Mesra states that candidates should have secured their bachelor’s degree from a recognised college/university with at least 50% to 55% marks in aggregate (5% relaxation for SC/ST candidates) , depending on the programme. Appearing students will also be eligible to apply.
