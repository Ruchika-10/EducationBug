Admission:

Eligibility Criteria for M.Phil. The candidate must have an LLM Degree or any Master’s Degree with 50% marks.
Eligibility Criteria for BA LLB {Hons.} The candidate must have passed 10+2 pattern with 45% of the total marks in case of general category and 40% of the total marks in case of SC and ST applicants. Age Limit - 20 years for GC and 21 for SC/ST candidates.
  Eligibility Criteria for LLM Applicant must be a 3-years Law graduate from any recognized University with at least 50% marks. Admission will also be based on the performance in the Written Test, experience, published articles
